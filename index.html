Hey guys!! Its so cool writing to you all on my first blog post ever, on my cool new personal blog page! Posted by:

Published on February 10, 2025

Hey again guys!!! Little bit of a different post than last week but who cares! This is my favorite car of all time! It’s the Bugatti Veyron 16.4, made by Bugatti in the mid 2000s and it instantly became my favorite car when I saw it for the first time. I think I saw it in a book about fast cars in elementary school for the first time, and since then it has always stuck with me. It has a 16 cylinder engine, (most normal cars have 3-6) and makes over a thousand horsepower!!! That’s literally insane. It can go from 0-60 in 2.6 seconds and it looks like a weird egg that was made to go as fast as possible. It sounds insane, like a raspier V8 engine but just better in every way. I have seen a couple in real life and every time I do it always makes me stop and stare. It was also in a ton of the videogames that I played when I was growing up, most notably to me was Need For Speed Shift, where there was a mission in the game that had you driving one of these down one of the hardest tracks in the world (it was a simulation type game) and I could never do it because of how fast the car was. This will always be my favorite.
